About the AI Moral Code

The AI Moral Code initiative synthesizes empirical AI ethics data and transforms it into operational moral frameworks. This site presents the Normative, Regulatory, Behavioral, and Conceptual (NRBC) architecture, which is grounded in the analysis of 291 ethics documents from 2006–2025.

Directed by Ran Hinrichs, the project advances the practical governance of AI systems by embedding trust, fairness, transparency, accountability, and non-maleficence directly into system architecture and organizational policy.
